Privacy first

Your essay is
yours. Always.

We built SafeGrade because we were uncomfortable with how other tools handle student work. Here's exactly what we do — and don't do.

  • Your data stays on UK servers
    Your essay is stored securely on UK-based infrastructure so you can access your history, compare drafts, and track your progress. It is never shared with third parties, never sent to Turnitin or any university system, and never used to train AI models.
  • Your privacy is protected
    SafeGrade has no connection to Turnitin, your university's VLE, or any institutional system. Your essay data is stored securely on UK servers and never shared with your institution or any third party. You can delete your data at any time from Account Settings.
  • UK-hosted infrastructure
    SafeGrade runs on UK-based hardware. Your data doesn't cross borders. GDPR-compliant by design.
  • Academic integrity safe
    Using SafeGrade is no different to using a spell checker. We're a review tool — not a contract cheating service.
