Writing Analysis — 6 dimensions
Perplexity, burstiness, vocabulary diversity, phrase patterns, sentence variety, paragraph structure. Shows how the writing reads across every signal.
Free & unlimited
Harvard & APA Citation Check
Validates in-text citations against the reference list. Catches missing sources, formatting errors, and mismatches before the marker does.
Free & unlimited
UK Grade Band Estimation
Estimates First, 2:1, 2:2, Third based on writing quality signals weighted for the student’s subject area and UK grade boundaries.
Free & unlimited
